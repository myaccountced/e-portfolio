City Events App

Click the Project URL above to see the pages or click the GitHub link above to see the repository. The primary goal of this project is to deliver an app based on the requirement of our client zu. They are a local software development company that agreed to worked with Saskpolytech's CST students as a client. The app shows all the events happening in the city, with a feature allowing users to post and manage events, and moderators that can manage the website.

This project demonstrate the ability to create a full stack (front-end and back-end) application. In the making of this project, we are required to collaborate with other team members and also the client. So, the successful implementation and deployment of the project meant that the communication and collaboration skills are used effectively.
